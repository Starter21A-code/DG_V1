HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Identify the country of origin where military equipment was designed and manufactured.

‚ö†Ô∏è Identify where it was BUILT, not who uses it!

Many countries operate equipment manufactured elsewhere. Your goal is to pinpoint the country that originally developed and produced the equipment, not the countries currently using it.

How to Play

1. View the Equipment

An image of military equipment will appear on your screen. Study it carefully - the visual design can often provide clues about its origin.

2. Make Your Guess

Click anywhere on the world map to drop a pin where you think the equipment was manufactured. You can also click on a specific country for more precision.

3. Confirm Your Selection

Once you've placed your pin, click "CONFIRM DROP" to submit your guess.

4. Bonus Round - Identify the Equipment

After guessing the location, you'll be challenged to identify the specific equipment from multiple choices. Correct identification earns bonus points!

Scoring

  • Location Accuracy: Up to 5,000 points based on how close your pin is to the actual manufacturing location
  • Equipment Identification: Additional 5,000 points for correctly naming the equipment
  • Total Possible: 10,000 points per round

The closer you click to the correct location, the more points you earn!

Game Modes

  • Start Game: Play 5 random rounds
  • Daily Challenge: Complete the same 5 equipment items as all other players today and compete on the leaderboard
  • Practice Hub: Browse and study all available equipment to improve your knowledge

Tips

  • Many countries produce distinctive equipment with unique design philosophies
  • Pay attention to details like shape, size, and visible markings
  • Western equipment often differs in design from Eastern equipment
  • Some countries are known for specific types of military technology

Good luck, and may your geographical knowledge be sharp!
