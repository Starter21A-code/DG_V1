Privacy Policy for Defence Guesser

Effective Date: 26 May 2026

Overview

Defence Guesser ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Defence Guesser application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of data when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, IP address, location or device identifiers. The only data transmitted off your device is the self-chosen callsign and Daily Challenge score that you optionally submit to the global leaderboard (see Cloud Leaderboard Data below).

Game Data and Scores (Local)

Game progress, high scores, Daily Challenge history, preferences, and your chosen callsign are stored locally on your device using standard web technologies (Local Storage). Local data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Cloud Leaderboard Data (Firebase)

The global Daily Challenge leaderboard is powered by Google Cloud Firestore (Firebase). Submitting a score is optional. When you choose to post a score, the following data is sent to and stored by Firebase:

  • Your chosen callsign (up to 16 characters — letters, numbers, hyphens and underscores only)
  • Your score for that day's Daily Challenge
  • A random installation ID (UUID) generated locally and stored in your browser's Local Storage. This is not a hardware identifier and is not linked to any account; it exists only to distinguish leaderboard entries from different devices that happen to choose the same callsign. Clearing site data resets it.
  • Date / week / month identifiers used for ranking
  • A server-side timestamp generated by Firebase at submission

No other personal information is sent. The callsign is freely chosen by you and need not identify you personally. Firebase Analytics is not enabled — only Cloud Firestore is used.

To request access to, or deletion of, any leaderboard entry you have submitted, email defenceguesser@gmail.com with the callsign(s) and approximate date(s) of submission. We action verified requests within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Leaflet – Interactive map library
  • CartoDB – Map tile provider
  • Google Fonts – Typography
  • Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore) – Cloud database for the global leaderboard

As Firebase's infrastructure provider, Google may log connection metadata at the network level when your browser communicates with Firestore, in accordance with the Firebase Privacy and Security Policy and the Google Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, contact us at defenceguesser@gmail.com.
